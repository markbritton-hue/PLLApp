How It Works

This application loads competition data from Google Sheets and displays it in an easy-to-read format. Here's how to get started:

  • First Time Setup: Visit the Setup page to configure your Google Sheets
  • Auto-Load: Once configured, all pages will automatically load data from your sheets
  • Live Updates: Data refreshes automatically every 10 seconds during competitions
  • Multiple Sheets: Supports Varsity, JV, and multiple platform sheets
